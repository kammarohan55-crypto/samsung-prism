# Acme Corp Benefits Package

## Health Insurance

### Medical Coverage
- **Provider**: BlueCross BlueShield PPO
- **Coverage**: Employee, spouse, and dependents up to age 26
- **Premium**: Company covers 90% of employee premium, 75% of dependent premium
- **Deductible**: $500 individual / $1,000 family
- **Out-of-pocket max**: $3,000 individual / $6,000 family
- **Enrollment**: Within 30 days of hire date or during annual open enrollment (November)

### Dental Coverage
- **Provider**: Delta Dental
- **Coverage**: Preventive care 100%, basic care 80%, major care 50%
- **Annual maximum**: $2,000 per person

### Vision Coverage
- **Provider**: VSP
- **Coverage**: Annual eye exam fully covered, $200 frame allowance every 2 years
- **Contact lens allowance**: $150/year

## Retirement

### 401(k) Plan
- **Company match**: 100% match on first 4% of salary, 50% match on next 2%
- **Vesting**: Immediate vesting on employee contributions, 3-year cliff vesting on employer match
- **Provider**: Fidelity Investments
- **Enrollment**: Eligible from day 1

## Professional Development

### Learning Budget
- **Annual budget**: $2,500 per employee for courses, conferences, and certifications
- **Approval**: Manager approval required for expenses over $500
- **Usage**: Books, online courses, conference tickets, certification exams

### Conference Attendance
- Up to 2 conferences per year with company sponsorship
- Additional days off for speaking engagements

## Wellness

### Gym Membership
- $75/month reimbursement for gym or fitness class memberships

### Mental Health
- Free access to BetterHelp platform for all employees and their families
- 6 free counseling sessions per year through EAP (Employee Assistance Program)

### Wellness Days
- 2 additional "wellness days" per year (separate from PTO and sick leave)
