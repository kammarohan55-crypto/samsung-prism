# Acme Corp Employee Handbook

## 1. Company Overview

### 1.1 Mission
Acme Corp builds intelligent tools that help teams work smarter. Our mission is to empower every team member to do their best work through technology and collaboration.

### 1.2 Values
- **Transparency**: We share information openly and communicate honestly.
- **Ownership**: We take responsibility for our work and its outcomes.
- **Growth**: We invest in learning and support each other's development.
- **Respect**: We treat everyone with dignity and value diverse perspectives.

## 2. Work Hours & Flexibility

### 2.1 Standard Hours
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM local time, Monday through Friday. However, we believe in flexibility — what matters is output, not hours logged.

### 2.2 Remote Work
All employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Fully remote arrangements are available for roles that don't require on-site presence, subject to manager approval.

### 2.3 Core Hours
Regardless of schedule flexibility, all team members should be available during core hours: 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM local time for meetings and collaboration.

## 3. Leave Policies

### 3.1 Paid Time Off (PTO)
- **Annual PTO**: 24 days per year for all full-time employees
- **Accrual**: PTO accrues at 2 days per month
- **Carryover**: Up to 5 unused PTO days may carry over to the next year
- **Request process**: Submit PTO requests at least 5 business days in advance via the HR portal

### 3.2 Sick Leave
- **Sick days**: 12 days per year
- **Doctor's note**: Required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days
- **Mental health days**: Sick leave explicitly covers mental health — no justification required

### 3.3 Parental Leave
- **Primary caregiver**: 16 weeks paid leave
- **Secondary caregiver**: 8 weeks paid leave
- **Eligibility**: After 6 months of continuous employment
- **Flexibility**: May be taken in blocks within 12 months of the qualifying event

### 3.4 Bereavement Leave
- **Immediate family**: 5 days paid leave
- **Extended family**: 3 days paid leave

### 3.5 Public Holidays
Acme Corp observes 10 public holidays per year. The holiday calendar is published at the start of each fiscal year.

## 4. Code of Conduct

### 4.1 Professional Behavior
All employees are expected to conduct themselves with professionalism, integrity, and respect. Harassment, discrimination, or retaliation of any kind is strictly prohibited.

### 4.2 Conflict of Interest
Employees must disclose any potential conflicts of interest to their manager and HR within 30 days.

### 4.3 Confidentiality
Company proprietary information, client data, and internal communications must be kept confidential. This obligation continues after employment ends.

## 5. IT & Security

### 5.1 Equipment
All employees receive a company laptop and necessary peripherals. Equipment must be returned upon departure.

### 5.2 Password Policy
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Changed every 90 days
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for all systems

### 5.3 Data Security
- Do not store company data on personal devices
- Use company VPN when accessing internal systems remotely
- Report security incidents to security@acme.com within 24 hours
